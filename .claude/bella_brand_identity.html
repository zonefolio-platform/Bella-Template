01 · Concept

The idea

A portfolio that feels like it was assembled by a human, not generated by a machine.

Name
Bella
"Where craft becomes visible."
Core premise
Bella is built around the feeling of a blank sheet — open, considered, and full of potential. Unlike Vault's atmospheric dark, Bella works in warm light: cream-white backgrounds, serif typography with italic rhythm, generous whitespace, and subtle grain texture. The structure is open and editorial. The accent color is the only bold decision — and it's yours.
Who it's for
Designers, art directors, illustrators, photographers, architects, and motion creatives. People who think in grids and feel uncomfortable with templates that look like templates. Bella doesn't feel AI-generated because it leans into imperfection — organic curves, italic weight shifts, and a layout that breathes rather than fills.
Visual personality
Warm, considered, editorial. Cormorant Garamond's italic creates rhythm and elegance at large scale. Cream-white surfaces with subtle grain texture feel printed, not rendered. The grid is visible but not rigid — elements breathe and shift. Feels like a Pentagram case study, not a SaaS landing page.

04 · Animation stack

08 · Voice & copy

Copy tone

Bella sounds like a senior creative talking to a peer — not pitching, not performing. Quiet confidence. No superlatives.

Write like this
Short, specific bio — what you do and where, in one sentence
Section labels in DM Mono — "Selected Work", "About", "Process"
Project names that describe the project, not the outcome
Email address at display size — no label, no intro text
Roles in italic serif — they read like disciplines, not job titles
Sentence case everywhere — always
Avoid this
"I'm a passionate creative who loves storytelling"
Skill progress bars — they're made-up numbers
Portfolio descriptions longer than two lines
All-caps section headers — sentence case only
Contact forms — a direct email is more human
Generic hero text like "Welcome to my work"

Before shipping

Every item must pass before Bella goes live in the template gallery.
